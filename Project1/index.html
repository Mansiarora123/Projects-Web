Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk (for kneading)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk gradually and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Make small smooth balls from the dough with no cracks.
  3. Heat oil on low-medium heat and fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Boil sugar and water together to make a thin syrup. Soak the fried balls in warm syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
